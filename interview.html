Historical Timeline of Interviews

1971 – On-Ground Reports

Journalists secretly recorded early evidence of genocide and military operations.

1972–1980 – Victim Testimonies

Large-scale documentation of torture survivors and refugee camp experiences.

1981–2000 – Freedom Fighter Interviews

Veterans sharing insights into tactical strategies, leadership, and cross-border missions.

2001–2010 – Digital Archiving Begins

First attempts to preserve war memories for the internet generation.

2011–Present – Modern Interview Preservation

High-quality video interviews with remaining freedom fighters and global researchers.
